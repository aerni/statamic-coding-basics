Everything is better when donuts are involved

Job interviews are painful. But with a pocketful of donuts they are never a waste of time. Hate waiting at the DMV? Try pounding a dozen donuts and splattering jelly all over form 90210. You're welcome.

15. April 2025

Sure they're not healthy for you, but neither is the stress you carry when you spend time doing things you hate. All of these things are better with donuts.

Sugar Diplomacy

Donuts have a way of resolving conflict that no formal process ever could. Passive-aggressive email from accounting? Show up with a pink box and suddenly everyone is "circling back" with a much better attitude.

Emotional Cushioning

Life insists on happening to you at full speed. Missed train. Weird text. Dentist appointment. The donut does not remove these burdens, but it does place a soft, frosted barrier between you and total collapse.

Tactical Celebration

Donuts are uniquely qualified for events that do not deserve a full cake but still require recognition. They arrive, do the work, and leave a faint dusting of sugar as proof that joy briefly existed.
