Mondays have become the universal scapegoat for problems they did not create. All of these things are better with donuts.

## Blame Culture

Monday didn’t ruin your life. It just showed up. The real issue is everything you postponed until now. But blaming a day is easier than confronting your decisions.

## Expectation Hangover

Weekends create unrealistic standards. Freedom, rest, control. Monday removes the illusion and people take it personally.

## Rebranding Opportunity

What if Monday wasn’t the enemy? What if it was just… neutral? Add a donut, lower your expectations, and suddenly it’s manageable. Not great, but survivable.
