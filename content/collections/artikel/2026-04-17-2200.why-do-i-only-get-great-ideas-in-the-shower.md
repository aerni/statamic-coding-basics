The most inconvenient brainstorming environment continues to outperform all others. All of these things are better with donuts.

## Peak Creativity Timing

Your brain waits until you are completely unavailable before doing its best work. No notebook. No phone. Just water and regret.

## The Illusion of Recall

You convince yourself the idea is too good to forget. You’re wrong. By the time you dry off, it’s gone or significantly worse.

## Post-Shower Reality

You emerge determined to capture brilliance next time. You won’t. But maybe you’ll have a donut nearby, and honestly, that’s still a win.
